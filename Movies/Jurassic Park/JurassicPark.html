Jurassic Park

Oh boy, I can't even count how many times I've watched this movie. I was but a wee lad when it came out and it scared the heck out of me. I didn't get to see in theaters originally but when it came out on tv, I would hide in the kitchen and peak around the corner to watch. Evnetually I overcame my fear and once I did I watched it all the time. We taped it on VHS and I would watch it, rewind it, watch it, rewind it. It was great! I could probably even repeat most of the movie line for line as it plays. My favorite scene in all of movies is when the T-Rex steps out of the cage and roars, simply amazing!
